      Privacy
      
    
  
      
  
      
    
      What is stored: the entries you type (medicine, dose, time, note) and, if you add one, the tracker name. Nothing else - no accounts, no analytics, no cookies, no third-party services.
      
    
      Where and for how long: on our server in the EU, reachable only through your tracker's random link. Everything is permanently erased 10 days after the last entry (60 days at most).
      
    
      Technical logs: like almost every website, the server keeps standard access logs (IP address and requested address) for up to 30 days, used only for protection against abuse.
      
    
      Your control: delete any entry yourself, or simply let the tracker expire - nothing is kept afterwards.
